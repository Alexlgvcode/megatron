# CAS4127 — Sample Course Syllabus

## Schedule
- Problem Set 1: due Week 2, Friday 11:59pm
- Problem Set 2: due Week 4, Friday 11:59pm
- Problem Set 3: due Week 6, Friday 11:59pm
- Midterm exam: Week 5, Tuesday in class
- Final project: due Week 12, Friday 11:59pm

## Grading
- Problem sets: 40%
- Midterm exam: 20%
- Final project: 30%
- Participation: 10%

## Late policy
Late problem sets are accepted up to 48 hours past the deadline, with a 10%
penalty per 24-hour block. After 48 hours, submissions are not accepted
without prior approval from the instructor.

## Submission
All assignments are submitted via the course LMS. PDF format only.

## Office hours
- Instructor: Tuesdays 2-4pm, Room 421
- TA: Thursdays 1-3pm, Room 311

## Extensions
Extension requests must be made via email at least 48 hours before the
deadline, and require a brief justification. Extensions are at the
instructor's discretion.

## Academic integrity
Collaboration on problem sets is encouraged for discussion, but every
student must write up their own solutions. Code must be your own.
